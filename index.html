Tell Me Muse Episode List

Tell Me Muse is a student-run podcast that interviews recent McGill
Classics graduates to answer the question “What is Classics?”

The discipline is wide and new research keeps expanding it. Join us as we
plunge into Ancient Greek and Roman literature, mythology, philosophy,
archaeology, and more!
